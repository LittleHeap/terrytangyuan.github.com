The more you give, the more you receive.

I will be joining H2O.AI as a senior AI platform engineer in December.

Before joining H2O.AI, I was a data scientist at Uptake. I led a small team of software engineers and data scientists to build our scalable data science platform to enable data scientists to build, deploy, and monitor our models that run in real-time at scale, monitoring the condition of IoT assets from industries like rail, aviation, and energy.

Meanwhile, I am an active contributor to numerous popular open-source softwares, including TensorFlow, XGBoost, etc. Please check out my projects page and Github page for more information. I am also an award-winning author of best-selling book TensorFlow in Practice which is the first book teaching TensorFlow in Chinese and has becoming training material for many companies and research labs in China.

I graduated from the Pennsylvania State University in Mathematics with honors. Since my senior year at Penn State, I've been a founding data scientist of DataNovo, a legal analytics company that provides predictive insights, keywordless prior art search, and product assessment on infringement for law firms, patent attorneys, etc.
